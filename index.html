Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment or do you take walk-ins?
While we do accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to guarantee your spot, especially on weekends and our extended Tuesday hours.
What is the difference between Gel and Acrylic?
Acrylics are a mix of liquid and powder that harden to create a strong extension over your natural nail. Gel polish is painted on like regular polish but cured under a UV light for a long-lasting, chip-free finish on your natural nails.
How do you sanitize your tools?
We strictly adhere to state board regulations. All metal implements are scrubbed, disinfected, and sterilized in a medical-grade autoclave. We also use disposable liners for all pedicure bowls and single-use files/buffers.
Do you offer nail art?
Yes! Our talented technicians can create a wide variety of nail art, from simple French tips and ombre to intricate hand-painted designs and 3D embellishments. Please mention nail art when booking so we allocate enough time.
